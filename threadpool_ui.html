How it works
1
submit() wraps your function in a packaged_task, pushes to priority queue (mutex held), returns a future instantly.
2
Workers sleep on queue_cv_. notify_one() wakes exactly one — no thundering herd.
3
Worker pops highest-priority task, releases mutex, then executes — lock-free parallelism.
4
future.get() blocks only at that moment. Exceptions inside tasks re-throw on .get().
5
wait_all() sleeps on idle_cv_ until active_count_ hits zero and queue is empty.
